Why I am inspired by Figure Skating

What is Figure Skating and why does it inspire me?

Figure Skating is a winter sport which combines atheletism and artistry, similar to gymnastics. It involves two events; the short program and free skate. Included in the program is a combination of jumps, spins, elements and artistic choreography. This page gives background about the sport to help visitors understand why I find these female skaters inspiring! Visit the Ladies Figure Skaters I find inspiring page in the nav bar.

Spins in Figure skating

In figure skating competition, each female skater has to perform multiple spins in each program. Each of these spins have different names and positions, and skaters often add their own twist to them (see images below).
